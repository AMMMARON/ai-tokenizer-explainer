AI Tokenizer Explainer

Large Language Models don't read words like humans do. They break text into smaller chunks called tokens. Type below to see how a simple whitespace and punctuation tokenizer works!

* Note: Real AI models (like GPT-4) use more complex algorithms like Byte Pair Encoding (BPE), but the concept of splitting text into chunks remains the same.

Why Tokenize?

  • Vocabulary Size: Instead of learning every unique word (like 'run', 'running', 'runner'), models learn common sub-parts.
  • Efficiency: Common words get their own tokens, while rare words are built from smaller pieces.
  • Numbers: Numbers are often split into individual digits or small groups.
